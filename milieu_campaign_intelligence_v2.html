Frequently Asked Questions

No. They are two levels of deployment under the same Milieu Campaign Intelligence architecture. They share the same module names, campaign journey logic, visual structure and commercial narrative. Clients choosing Lite are not buying a lesser version — they are accessing the same system with a simpler entry configuration.
Lite is the fast-entry version of Milieu Campaign Intelligence. It gives clients access to pre-built modules with simpler setup, faster turnaround (24–48 hours) and an easy, fixed per-module buying logic. It is designed for agencies, new business teams and fast-moving client teams who need consumer insight quickly without a complex procurement process.
Expanded is the broader, deeper version of the same system. It allows for greater flexibility, fuller coverage across the campaign cycle, richer diagnostics, and stronger integration with wider Milieu capabilities including Portraits, BrandVantage and brand tracking. It is designed for established clients, multi-market teams and strategic programmes where continuity and depth matter.

The architecture stays constant; the depth increases. This means clients deepen their use of the same Milieu system rather than buying a different product.
